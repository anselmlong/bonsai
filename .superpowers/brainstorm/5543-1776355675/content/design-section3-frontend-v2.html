Urban real estate markets
Summary
Remote work has fundamentally altered urban real estate dynamics. Office vacancy in major metros reached historic highs — 22.7% in Manhattan, 28% in San Francisco — while suburban residential prices surged 34% from 2020–2024. Cities are now pursuing commercial-to-residential conversion, but progress is constrained by zoning restrictions and structural incompatibility of office floorplates.
